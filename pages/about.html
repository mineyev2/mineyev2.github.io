My name is Roman, currently a Junior undergraduate at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

My first introduction to music was when my parents enrolled me for piano practice. However, relatable to many other kids, after a couple years I quit.

My excitement for music sparked back during my sophomore year of highschool. I picked up the piano again after watching Kyle Landry's rendition of Howl's Moving Castle on YouTube (If you haven't seen it you definitely should check it out!).

Ever since then music has always been something I truly identify with, and I hope that all of you that find this website enjoy the beats and melodies that I have produced!
